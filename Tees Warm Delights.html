Our story

A small kitchen with big flavour.

Tee's Warm Delights started the way most good things do — at home, at the kitchen table, with flour on the counter and someone asking for "just one more". Today we bake to order for birthdays, tea trays, office mornings, and the quiet moments in between.

Every scone is rolled by hand. Every cake is layered from scratch. Nothing sits on a shelf — we bake the day you collect, so every bite is still warm.

— TeeOwner & head baker

Questions

Things people often ask.

How do I place an order?
The easiest way is WhatsApp — send us a message with what you'd like, how many, and when you need it for. You can also call or fill in the contact form below. We'll reply with a quote and confirm everything before you pay.
How much notice do you need?
For scones, biscuits, and cookies — 24 hours. For cakes, rolls, and larger platters — 48 hours. For big events (50+ people), please give us at least a week so we can plan properly.
Do you deliver?
Yes — we deliver locally around Johannesburg for a small fee (depending on distance). Collection is free from our kitchen. Let us know your suburb when you order and we'll sort the rest.
How do I pay?
We accept EFT and cash on collection. A small deposit secures larger orders (cakes, platters). Full bank details are shared when we confirm your order.
Can you cater for allergies or halaal?
All our ingredients are halaal-friendly. For nut allergies, please let us know up front — we bake with nuts in the kitchen, so we'll advise on what we can safely accommodate.
Do you cater events?
Yes — tea tables, dessert tables, office mornings, weddings, and kitchen teas. Send us the date, headcount, and any theme colours and we'll put a proposal together.
